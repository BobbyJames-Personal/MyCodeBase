No Place Like Home Pet Services

Our History

Shari's No Place like Home Pet Services started in 2013 with me watching pets of clients from the Veterinary Practice where I worked. Over the years this evolved into a real business. We are a family run business and are insured and bonded. Our Mission is to provide quality care for your pets while you are away and keep the pets in their own home environement.

Qualifications

Being a certified Veterinary Professional allows me the ability to deal with any situation that may arise. I am adept at giving medications including insulin. I have a true understanding of animal behavior and am adept at putting them at ease.
